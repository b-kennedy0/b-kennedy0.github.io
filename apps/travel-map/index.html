    Setup

    Connect your published Google Sheet

    Add your published CSV URL to the sheetCsvUrl setting in apps/travel-map/app.js.

    After adding or renaming places in the sheet, regenerate the local place-boundary file with node apps/travel-map/scripts/generate-place-boundaries.mjs.

    The app supports either of these sheet formats:

    Preferred schema

    • Name
    • City/Area
    • Country
    • Year
    • Visited By

    Current compact schema

    • Name as visitor name, for example Brad, Ryan, or Both
    • Area as the place label
    • Country
    • Year

    Accepted Visited By values are Me, Partner, and Both.
