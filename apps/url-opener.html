Troubleshooting

> Only ONE tab opened / No tabs opened?

If you are unable to open multiple URLs, please check your browser settings. Most modern browsers have a built-in popup blocker that may prevent multiple tabs from opening at once. You can usually find this setting in the browser's privacy or security settings.

Data Security

The links you paste into this app are NOT saved. This app runs in your browser window, as such, the data is not collect or stored. If you'd like to verify this for yourself, click to view the source code.
